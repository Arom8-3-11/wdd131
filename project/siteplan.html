In most succeful sites I have looked at before, the more succeful sites generally only use a color pallette of 2-3 colors with the exception of black and white for nuetral colors.

Deep Brown/Burgandy and Apricot are two colors tend to be very associated with food and cooking. these two colors also provide a warm/at home feel in which generally will provide them with comfort.
